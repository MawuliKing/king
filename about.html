BACKGROUND

Adwinton is an online marketing platform established by 12 students of University for Development Studies (UDS) led by Yahya Awal in 2020 during their stay on campus for the second trimester of the school academic year in Ghana. The Team main aim was to help create ready market for agriculture produce and service by linking sellers to buyers and also to facilitate business transaction between individuals, small and medium enterprises and companies or factories. The team comprises of Students from agriculture field. This idea occurred to Yahya Awal, when he observed that most of the farmers lose their income and investment through post harvest losses due to lack of ready market for their produce. Mostly on his way from lectures farmers into pepper farming complains of how the middle men cheat them due to lack of ready market for it. Aside that, he also observed that post harvest loss of watermelon in the community is always high.Awal once took pictures of bagged pepper, Peanuts, Maize, Soya bean, Yam and created an online ad to market them on Jiji which is more into marketing of accessories. People started calling him each passing day if he could supply them with such produce in large quantity. A partner from FOODTECH company in Accra met Awal on Campus for him to link him with peanut sellers and to also help him purchase 6mt of Peanut when Awal disclose to him that He only created the ad to see how people will patronized e-commerce in Agriculture. After helping the man, many more people were still contacting him to supply them with such goods but Awal could not due to lack of capital. He tried to get loan from Banks to make a purchase to supply but no Bank in Tamale could grant him because he was not a salary earner. This click to his mind that, why not create a legitimate online market platform where sellers of agriculture products could display their products and market it to the world. With this idea in mind he started looking for His colleague students who will be willing to take part in such a project. He talked to business minded guys and those who are also into IT engineering and ready to help come out with their online marketing website.


HOW SERIOUS IS POST HARVEST LOSS IN AFRICA?


  • According to the Food and Agriculture Organisation of the United Nations, 30% of food produced for human consumption is lost or wasted along the supply chain every year. This is a whopping 1.3 billion metric tons of food that doesnâ€™t ever reach the consumer. Some reports have estimated that this lost or wasted food could be used to feed 1.6 billion people every year.
  • In Africa, the losses are even higher: between 30% and 50%. They occur mainly downstream, between the production and retail stages of the supply chain. Fruit and vegetable losses are estimated to be 50% or more.

VISION

Our team have a common belief that about 80% of the world population will buy and sell their goods and services by leveraging on logistic online marketing platforms by 2045. As the internet penetration rate in developing countries keeps on increasing. Agriculture could be a major role player in future e-commerce especially in developing countries even though the sector has been underexplored by the internet industry.

GOALS

  • Create a suitable platform which will help business men and women in agriculture to market their goods and service globally at minimum cost requirement.
  • To help create employment to over 50% of Africa population through the promotion of agriculture e-commerce.
  • To make more than 5000tons goods delivery per day by 2025.
  • To help create food security among Africa countries.
  • To help sustain Africa agriculture processing industries by supplying them with the required raw materials and distribution of their goods.
  • To Empower small and medium agriculture business enterprises to experience globalization
  • To become the best agriculture online market in the world by 2045.

TARGET MARKET & POTENTIAL MARKET

  • Farmers
  • Restaurants
  • Small and medium agriculture marketers
  • Poultry farms
  • Animal husbandry
  • Processing industries and companies
  • Exporters
  • Individuals

OUR SERVICES

  • To help individuals, small and medium enterprises to create online shops to market their agriculture goods and service globally at low cost. We also help you deliver or make an order at your comfort zone.
  • Our Business Model is B2B2C: our company will engage in the company to wholesale to retail business transactions. We will supply and distribute raw materials and processed agriculture materials respectfully. We will also help create ready market for small and medium agriculture enterprises.
